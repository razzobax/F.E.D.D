About Us

Innovative Delivery Solutions: F.E.D.D. is a Srl founded by four students from ISII Marconi in Piacenza. Our company specializes in drone delivery services for e-commerce platforms, offering efficient, eco-friendly, and high-quality delivery solutions with zero emissions.

  • Reda Razouk: Chief Operating Officer, Designer, Web Dev.
  • Diego Grazioli: Commercial Director, Info Gathering.
  • Paolo Bonini: Financial Manager, Decision Helper.
  • Giovanni De Simone: Human Resources Manager, Locator.

What can we offer

We offer a zero-emission drone delivery service that is fast, secure, and especially designed to leverage specific lockers, enabling deliveries even in densely populated urban areas. Customers can conveniently retrieve their packages released by the drone near their homes.

Why Revolutionary

Driven by the imperative of 'SAVING THE PLANET,' we have devised a delivery service with zero emissions, striving to offer a sustainable solution in line with our environmental ethos. Our service effectively nullifies the consumption of gasoline for a 30 km route, thus eliminating 21 kg of CO2 emissions typically expelled during the journey.
